# Glossary of Terms

- **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.
- **Financial Inclusion:** The availability and equality of opportunities to access financial services.
- **SME / MSME:** Small and Medium Enterprises / Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.
- **A2F:** Access to Financial Services (survey by EFInA).
- **GDP:** Gross Domestic Product.
- **Digital Readiness:** The preparedness of a country's infrastructure, government, and population to adopt and utilize digital technologies effectively.
